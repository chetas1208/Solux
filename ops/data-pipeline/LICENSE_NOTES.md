## ODbL (OpenStreetMap) Obligations

Any dataset containing OSM-derived data must:
1. Credit OpenStreetMap contributors.
2. Keep OSM-derived layers under ODbL (share-alike applies to the database).
3. Not use OSM data in ways that restrict user access to the underlying database.

## Important Restrictions

- **JRC Global Surface Water**: Free for non-commercial research. Commercial use requires separate agreement with EC JRC.
- **GRanD**: Research-only license. Not approved for commercial redistribution without contacting the Global Dam Watch team.
- **NITI Aayog Energy Map**: Government of India restricted portal — no bulk download. Only public metadata is cited.
- **Copernicus DEM**: Attribution required per ESA/DLR terms. Derivatives must carry attribution.
